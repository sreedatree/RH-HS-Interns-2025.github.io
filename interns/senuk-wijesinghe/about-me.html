About Me

I'm a student going to Apex Friendship High School and I am focusing my efforts on learning all forms of development. I specifically focus on low-level development utilizing C, C++, and Assembly. I also am working on learning the intricacies of web development, design, and game development. I hope to make something that can be used for good in the future.

Some of my hobbies include music and drawing. I love drawing more brutalist art using pens, and I am a big fan of improvisation in jazz. I play the baritone saxophone, and I also march the sousaphone. I am a big DCI fanboy and love to rate and compare shows.
