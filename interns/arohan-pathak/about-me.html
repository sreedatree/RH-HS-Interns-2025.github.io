About Me

I'm a rising high school Senior from Littleton, Massachussetts, with an interest in engineering and business. I like learning new things, meeting new people, and building new connections. I've lived in Mass my entire life, and have always been attracted to the field of engineering, primarily because of my consistent interest in cars. As a result I started robotics when I was in 6th grade in FIRST Lego League (FLL), and joined an FIRST Robotics Team (FRC) team in 8th grade. Since then I have been part of the team and have learned a series of valuable skills ranging from Mechanical, Electrical, Software, Business, Fundraising, and a bunch of other very important skills.
